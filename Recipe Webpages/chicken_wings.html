Sticky Honey Chicken Wings

Sweet and sticky honey-glazed chicken wings with a tangy punch from white vinegar and a smoky depth from paprika.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 25 minutes (plus 4 hours marinating)
  2. Cook Time: 30 minutes
  3. Total Time: Approximately 5 hours
  4. Yield: About 3 dozen wings
  5. Difficulty: Easy
  6. Cuisine: American

Ingredients

  • ½ cup orange blossom honey
  • ⅓ cup white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 pounds chicken wings

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine honey, white vinegar, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cut through the two wing joints with a sharp knife, discarding wing tips. Add remaining wing pieces and honey mixture to a large bowl; stir to coat. Cover and refrigerate 4 hours or overnight.
  3. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Remove wings; reserve honey mixture.
  4. Place wings on greased 15x10x1-inch baking pans. Bake until juices run clear, about 30 minutes, turning halfway through.
  5. Meanwhile, place reserved honey mixture in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil; cook 1 minute.
  6. Remove wings from oven; preheat broiler. Place wings on a greased rack in a broiler pan; brush with honey mixture.
  7. Broil 4-5 inches from heat until crispy, 3-5 minutes.
  8. Serve with remaining honey mixture.
