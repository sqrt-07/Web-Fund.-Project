Italian Tuna Salad

A fresh and flavorful Italian-inspired tuna salad with crisp vegetables, tender tuna chunks, and a zesty dressing — perfect for a light lunch or picnic.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 15 minutes
  2. Servings: 4
  3. Difficulty: Easy
  4. Cuisine: Italian

Ingredients

  • 1 (5-ounce) can good quality tuna packed in olive oil, drained
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: Crusty bread, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, gently flake the tuna with a fork.
  2. Add cherry tomatoes, cucumber, roasted red peppers, olives, red onion, and basil to the bowl.
  3. Drizzle the olive oil and red wine vinegar over the salad.
  4. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  5. Toss gently to combine without breaking up the tuna too much.
  6. Serve immediately with crusty bread if desired.
