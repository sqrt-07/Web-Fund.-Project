Mussels with Fennel and Italian Beer

Fresh mussels steamed to perfection with aromatic fennel, garlic, and bright Italian beer — a light yet flavorful seafood dish ideal for any occasion.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 15 minutes
  2. Cook Time: 10 minutes
  3. Total Time: 25 minutes
  4. Servings: 4
  5. Difficulty: Easy
  6. Cuisine: Italian

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 fennel bulb, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 2 pounds fresh mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • 1 bottle (12 oz) Italian lager or pale ale beer
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped, plus more for garnish
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Lemon wedges, for serving
  • Crusty bread, for soaking up broth

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add sliced fennel and garlic; sauté until softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and stir for 30 seconds.
  4. Add mussels and pour in the beer. Cover the pot and cook until mussels open, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Discard any mussels that remain closed.
  6. Stir in chopped parsley, season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve immediately with lemon wedges and crusty bread to soak up the flavorful broth.
