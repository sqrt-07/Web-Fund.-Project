Butter Chicken

Creamy, tomato-based chicken curry simmered with aromatic spices and finished with butter and cream — a quintessential Indian comfort dish loved worldwide.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 20 minutes
  2. Marinate Time: 1 hour to overnight
  3. Cook Time: 30 minutes
  4. Total Time: Approx. 2 hours
  5. Servings: 4-6
  6. Difficulty: Medium
  7. Cuisine: Indian

Ingredients

  • 500 g boneless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken pieces, yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder. Marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  2. In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger; sauté until golden.
  3. Add marinated chicken (discard excess marinade) and cook until chicken is no longer pink.
  4. Stir in tomato puree, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Reduce heat, stir in cream, and simmer uncovered for another 5-10 minutes until sauce thickens.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.
