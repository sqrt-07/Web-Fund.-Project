Tandoori Chicken

Juicy boneless chicken thighs marinated in a fragrant blend of yogurt and Indian spices, oven-broiled to tender perfection with a smoky, vibrant crust — a beloved Indian classic.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 15 minutes
  2. Marinate Time: 1 hour to overnight
  3. Cook Time: 15 minutes
  4. Total Time: Approx. 2 hours
  5. Servings: 4
  6. Difficulty: Medium
  7. Cuisine: Indian

Ingredients

  • 5 whole cloves
  • 2 dried guajillo chiles (or 2 chiles de arbol)
  • 2 green cardamom pods, husks removed, seeds retained
  • 1 black cardamom pod, husk removed, seeds retained
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ½ teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 cup whole milk yogurt (plain, full fat)
  • ¼ cup peanut or canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons malt vinegar or lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika
  • ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • Pinch cayenne pepper
  • 8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-inch piece ginger, peeled and minced
  • 1.5 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Extra lime wedges, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Toast cloves, dried chiles, cardamoms, coriander seeds, fennel seeds, and fenugreek seeds in a dry skillet over medium heat until fragrant, about 3 minutes. Grind spices into a fine powder using a spice grinder.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the ground spices with yogurt, oil, malt vinegar, salt, cinnamon, paprika, turmeric, cayenne pepper, garlic, and ginger until well combined and aromatic.
  3. Reserve ⅓ cup of the marinade for serving.
  4. Prick chicken thighs with a fork and toss in the remaining marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.
  5. Preheat broiler and line a baking sheet with foil. Place chicken thighs on the sheet; discard excess marinade but coat chicken evenly.
  6. Broil for 5 minutes until starting to char, then reduce oven heat to 350°F and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C).
  7. While chicken cooks, heat reserved marinade with honey and ½ cup water in a small saucepan over medium-low heat until gently boiling; whisk until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Serve chicken hot with the sauce and lime wedges on the side.
