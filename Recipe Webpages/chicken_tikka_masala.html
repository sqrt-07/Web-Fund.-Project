Chicken Tikka Masala

Tender chicken chunks marinated in spiced yogurt, grilled and simmered in a creamy, tomato-infused masala sauce — a globally beloved Indian dish with rich, aromatic flavors.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 30 minutes
  2. Marinate Time: 1 hour to overnight
  3. Cook Time: 25 minutes
  4. Total Time: Approx. 2 hours
  5. Servings: 4
  6. Difficulty: Medium
  7. Cuisine: Indian

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt, to taste
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, cayenne, and salt.
  2. Add chicken chunks, coat well, cover, and marinate for 1 hour or overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat grill or oven to 450°F (230°C). Thread chicken onto skewers and grill or bake for 10-12 minutes, turning halfway, until cooked through.
  4. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and ginger, sauté until golden and fragrant.
  5. Add tomato puree, cook for 10 minutes stirring occasionally.
  6. Stir in cream and cooked chicken pieces. Simmer 5 minutes until sauce thickens and chicken is well coated.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
