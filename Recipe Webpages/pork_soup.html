Pork Soup Dumplings

Delicate, steamed pork dumplings filled with a savory broth that bursts in your mouth — a Chinese dim sum favorite lovingly crafted at home.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 1 hour 15 minutes (including dough and filling prep)
  2. Cook Time: 15 minutes steaming
  3. Total Time: Approx. 1 hour 30 minutes
  4. Servings: 24 dumplings
  5. Difficulty: Medium
  6. Cuisine: Chinese, Dim Sum

Ingredients

  • For the dough:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup boiling water
  • For the filling:
  • 12 oz ground pork
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine (or dry sherry)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 3 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup hot chicken broth (for soup gelatin)
  • 1 tablespoon unflavored gelatin powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix gelatin powder with hot chicken broth and stir until dissolved. Refrigerate until set into a firm jelly.
  2. Prepare dough by mixing flour and boiling water, knead into a smooth ball. Cover and let rest 30 minutes.
  3. Dice the gelatin jelly into small cubes.
  4. In a bowl, combine ground pork, soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, sesame oil, green onions, ginger, sugar, salt, and pepper. Gently fold in the gelatin cubes.
  5. Roll dough into a long log, cut into 24 pieces. Roll each piece into a thin circle wrapper.
  6. Place about 1 tablespoon filling in the center of each wrapper. Pleat edges to seal the dumpling tightly.
  7. Steam dumplings over boiling water for about 15 minutes until cooked through.
  8. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
