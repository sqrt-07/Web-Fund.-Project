Easy Key Lime Pie

A refreshingly tangy no-bake Key lime pie with a creamy filling of cream cheese and sweetened condensed milk in a crunchy graham cracker crust.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 10 minutes
  2. Chill Time: At least 4 hours
  3. Total Time: 4+ hours
  4. Servings: 8 servings
  5. Difficulty: Very Easy
  6. Cuisine: American

Ingredients

  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup Key lime juice or regular lime juice
  • 1 graham cracker crust (9 inches)
  • 2 cups whipped topping
  • Optional: lime slices for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth.
  2. Beat in sweetened condensed milk and lime juice until blended.
  3. Pour filling into the graham cracker crust and cover.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight until set.
  5. Just before serving, garnish with whipped topping and lime slices if desired.
