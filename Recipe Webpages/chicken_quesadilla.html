Best Chicken Quesadilla

Crispy golden tortillas stuffed with seasoned chicken, gooey melted cheese, and your favorite Mexican toppings — a cheesy crowd-pleaser any night of the week!

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 15 minutes
  2. Cook Time: 10 minutes
  3. Total Time: 25 minutes
  4. Servings: 4
  5. Difficulty: Easy
  6. Cuisine: Mexican

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 teaspoon taco seasoning
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Sour cream, salsa, and guacamole, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix chicken with taco seasoning and green onions.
  2. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat. Place a tortilla in the pan, sprinkle with 1/4 chicken mixture and cheese, then top with another tortilla.
  3. Cook until golden, flip, and cook the other side. Repeat with remaining tortillas and filling.
  4. Slice into wedges and serve with sour cream, salsa, and guacamole.
