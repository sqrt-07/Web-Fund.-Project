Best Shrimp Tacos

Crispy spiced shrimp wrapped in warm tortillas, topped with zesty slaw and creamy sauce — these shrimp tacos are a fiesta for your tastebuds!

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 15 minutes
  2. Cook Time: 10 minutes
  3. Total Time: 25 minutes
  4. Servings: 4
  5. Difficulty: Easy
  6. Cuisine: Mexican

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 small jalapeño, minced
  • Fresh cilantro, for serving

Instructions

  1. Toss shrimp with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. In a skillet over medium-high, cook shrimp 2-3 minutes per side, until opaque.
  3. Mix cabbage, mayo, lime juice, and jalapeño for the slaw.
  4. Warm tortillas; assemble each with slaw, shrimp, and cilantro.
