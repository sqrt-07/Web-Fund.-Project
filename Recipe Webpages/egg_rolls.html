Takeout Style Air Fryer Egg Rolls

Crispy, golden-brown egg rolls made easy in the air fryer, filled with seasoned pork and crunchy vegetables for a healthier take on a popular takeout favorite.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 15 minutes
  2. Cook Time: 10 minutes
  3. Total Time: 25 minutes
  4. Servings: 6 servings
  5. Difficulty: Easy
  6. Cuisine: American, Chinese-inspired

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 12 egg roll wrappers
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook ground pork until no longer pink; drain excess fat.
  2. Add cabbage, green onions, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, salt, and pepper; cook until cabbage is tender, about 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  3. Place about 2 tablespoons of pork mixture in the center of each egg roll wrapper. Fold in sides and roll tightly, sealing edges with water.
  4. Preheat the air fryer to 400°F (200°C). Spray egg rolls lightly with cooking spray.
  5. Arrange egg rolls in a single layer in the air fryer basket. Cook for 8-10 minutes turning halfway through, until golden and crispy.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
