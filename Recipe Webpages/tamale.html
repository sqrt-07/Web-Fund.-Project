Best Tamales

Fluffy masa dough parcels filled with seasoned meat, wrapped in corn husks and steamed — the ultimate Mexican comfort food for holidays and celebrations.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 1 hour
  2. Cook Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
  3. Total Time: 2 hours 30 minutes
  4. Servings: 20 tamales
  5. Difficulty: Medium
  6. Cuisine: Mexican

Ingredients

  • 3 cups masa harina
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup lard or vegetable shortening
  • 2 cups cooked, shredded beef or chicken
  • 1 cup salsa or enchilada sauce
  • 20 dried corn husks, soaked and drained

Instructions

  1. Combine masa harina, broth, baking powder, and salt. Beat in lard until fluffy.
  2. Spread 2 tablespoons masa dough on each corn husk. Add 1 tablespoon filling (meat mixed with salsa), fold and roll up the husks, folding the bottom up.
  3. Steam tamales upright in a steamer for 1.5 hours or until dough is firm.
  4. Let cool slightly before unwrapping. Enjoy warm!
