Sautéed Snow Pea Tips

Tender snow pea tips quickly sautéed with garlic and ginger for a light, nutritious side that complements every meal.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 10 minutes
  2. Cook Time: 5 minutes
  3. Total Time: 15 minutes
  4. Servings: 2-3 servings
  5. Difficulty: Easy
  6. Cuisine: Asian, Chinese-inspired

Ingredients

  • 1 lb snow pea tips, washed and trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and ginger; sauté until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  3. Add snow pea tips and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Drizzle soy sauce and season with salt and pepper; toss to coat evenly.
  5. Remove from heat and serve immediately as a fresh side dish.
