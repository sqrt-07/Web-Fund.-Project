Italian Fruit Toast

A delightful and colorful Italian-inspired toast topped with fresh seasonal fruits, creamy ricotta, honey, and a sprinkle of crunchy pistachios — perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 10 minutes
  2. Servings: 2
  3. Difficulty: Very Easy
  4. Cuisine: Italian

Ingredients

  • 4 slices rustic Italian bread
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon honey, plus more for drizzling
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mixed fresh fruit (e.g., berries, peaches, figs)
  • 1/4 cup shelled pistachios, roughly chopped
  • Fresh mint leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crisp.
  2. In a small bowl, mix ricotta cheese with honey and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Spread the ricotta mixture evenly on each slice of toast.
  4. Top with an assortment of fresh fruit.
  5. Drizzle additional honey over the fruit and sprinkle with chopped pistachios.
  6. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and serve immediately.
