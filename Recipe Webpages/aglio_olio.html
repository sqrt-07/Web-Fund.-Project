Aglio e Olio with Peas and Prosciutto

A delightful twist on classic aglio e olio pasta featuring sweet peas and salty prosciutto for a delicious, quick Italian meal.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 10 minutes
  2. Cook Time: 15 minutes
  3. Total Time: 25 minutes
  4. Servings: 4
  5. Difficulty: Easy
  6. Cuisine: Italian

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti or linguine
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • 4 ounces prosciutto, sliced and chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water then drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and cook gently until fragrant but not browned, about 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add prosciutto and peas, cook another 2-3 minutes until peas are tender.
  5. Stir in red pepper flakes if using.
  6. Add drained pasta to skillet and toss to coat. Add reserved pasta water as needed to loosen sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with Parmesan and chopped parsley before serving.
