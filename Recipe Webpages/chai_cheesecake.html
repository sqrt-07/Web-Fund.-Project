Mini Chai Cheesecakes with Parle-G Crusts and Salted Caramel

Delightful mini cheesecakes featuring fragrant chai spices nestled on buttery Parle-G cookie crusts, topped with decadent salted caramel — a perfect bite-sized treat.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 25 minutes
  2. Chill Time: 4 hours or overnight
  3. Total Time: Approx. 4 hours 30 minutes
  4. Yield: 12 mini cheesecakes
  5. Difficulty: Medium
  6. Cuisine: Fusion, Indian-inspired

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups crushed Parle-G cookies (about 20 cookies)
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon chai spice mix (cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, cloves, black pepper)
  • Salted caramel sauce, for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. Mix crushed Parle-G cookies with melted butter until well combined. Press about 1 tablespoon into each liner to form crust.
  3. Bake crusts for 8-10 minutes, then remove from oven.
  4. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla and chai spices.
  5. Pour cream cheese mixture over each crust, filling cups almost full.
  6. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until centers are just set. Remove and cool completely.
  7. Chill in refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Before serving, drizzle each mini cheesecake with salted caramel sauce.
