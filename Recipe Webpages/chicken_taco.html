Best Grilled Chicken Street Tacos

Tender, juicy grilled chicken bites packed in corn tortillas and topped with fresh onion, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime — the essence of Mexican street food!

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 15 minutes + marinating
  2. Cook Time: 15 minutes
  3. Total Time: 30 minutes + marinating
  4. Servings: 4
  5. Difficulty: Easy
  6. Cuisine: Mexican

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Corn tortillas
  • Finely chopped white onion and cilantro, for topping
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lime juice, garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, and salt. Mix in chicken pieces and marinate at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat grill or grill pan to medium-high. Grill chicken 4-5 minutes per side, until cooked through. Let rest, then chop into small pieces.
  3. Warm tortillas, fill with grilled chicken, top with onion and cilantro. Serve with lime wedges.
