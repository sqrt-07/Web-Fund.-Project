All American Banana Split

A nostalgic and delightful classic dessert featuring a split banana crowned with scoops of vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry ice cream, topped with fresh fruit, nuts, whipped cream, and cherries.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 10 minutes
  2. Cook Time: No cooking required
  3. Total Time: 10 minutes
  4. Servings: 2 servings
  5. Difficulty: Easy
  6. Cuisine: American

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana, peeled and split lengthwise
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1 scoop chocolate ice cream
  • 1 scoop strawberry ice cream
  • 2 tablespoons sliced fresh strawberries or strawberry ice cream topping
  • 2 tablespoons pineapple chunks or pineapple ice cream topping
  • 2 tablespoons whipped cream
  • 1 tablespoon chopped peanuts
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate syrup
  • 1 tablespoon caramel sundae syrup
  • 2 maraschino cherries with stems

Instructions

  1. Place the three scoops of ice cream in a dessert dish in a row.
  2. Arrange the banana halves on each side of the ice cream, letting the ice cream sit in the middle.
  3. Top with sliced strawberries, pineapple chunks, whipped cream, chopped peanuts, chocolate syrup, caramel sundae syrup, and cherries.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy the nostalgic delight.
