Chinese Sausage Fried Rice

Fragrant fried rice loaded with sweet and savory Chinese sausage, fresh vegetables, and eggs — a quick and satisfying one-pan meal.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 10 minutes
  2. Cook Time: 10 minutes
  3. Total Time: 20 minutes
  4. Servings: 4 servings
  5. Difficulty: Easy
  6. Cuisine: Chinese-inspired

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Chinese sausage (lap cheong), thinly sliced
  • 3 cups cooked jasmine rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add Chinese sausage slices and cook until fragrant and starting to brown, about 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  3. Push sausage to one side and pour beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble eggs until just set, then mix with sausage.
  4. Add cooked rice, frozen peas and carrots, and soy sauce. Stir well to combine and heat through.
  5. Stir in chopped green onions. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve warm as a main course or side dish.
