Tachin

A golden saffron-infused rice terrine layered with tender, spiced chicken and velvety yogurt, crowned with a crisp, caramelized crust — an elegant Persian classic celebrating harmony in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 tsp saffron (dissolved)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken (shredded)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp barberries
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil or Butter

Instructions

  1. Parboil rice, drain, and set aside.
  2. Mix yogurt, egg yolk, saffron, salt, and pepper with half the rice.
  3. Spread the yogurt and rice mixture in a greased pot.
  4. Layer sautéed chicken (cooked with onion, salt, and pepper) over the rice mixture.
  5. Top with the remaining plain rice.
  6. Cook on low heat for 1 hour.
  7. Separately, sauté barberries with sugar in butter.
  8. Sprinkle the sautéed barberries on top before serving.

Recipe Info

  1. Prep Time: 15 minutes
  2. Cook Time: 1hr 5 minutes
  3. Total Time: 1hr 20 minutes
  4. Servings: 9-12 people
  5. Difficulty: Easy
  6. Cuisine: Persian
